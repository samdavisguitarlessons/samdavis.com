Guitar Student
 
The high point of my week is walking into Sam Davis' studio, slipping off my shoes, sitting down and saying, "Hello, Sam." And he says, "Hello, Matt." And then he begins to unwind the mysteries of the guitar and make the instrument bigger than I ever thought it was to the point where I say, "Oh, my god!" And he says, I know you're thinking "Oh, my god!" right now, but don't worry, and here's why. Then he meticulously and humorously and effectively writes everything down so I can understand it and explains it to me and so I leave, my book full of Sam's knowledge, slip on my shoes, walk out the door and say, "Ah!"
