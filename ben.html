Performing Guitar Student
 

Sam Davis has been teaching guitar for something like 20 years. And one might expect that he's burnt out, but for some reason he's not, and I think I've figured it out - that he's so freakin' creative that he finds new ways to teach the same old stuff, and it's actually exciting for him when he realizes this, and so every lesson he comes into it with sort of a new look on things. And given his creativity, it will rub off on you and if it doesn't you should become an accountant cause you must be dull as shit.
